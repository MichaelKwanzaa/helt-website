Getting Started

What is Helt?

Helt offers health subscription services to customers. You can use Helt to pay for products or services that you utilize often to keep up with your productivity and health goals. Also, it gives you the latest discounts on health products around you and health tips on the go. These subscriptions are offered by partnerships with local vendors.

How do I create an account?

  • Download the Helt App
  • Open the app
  • Click “Get started”
  • You'll be asked to “Sign up”
  • Click “Sign up” and provide your information
  • Verify your email
  • Review the User Agreement and Privacy Notice then click “Agree”

Where is Helt supported?

At the moment, the Helt app is available only in Nigeria. If you will like to see Helt supported in your country you can send us a message at hello@heltapp.io Opening an account in another country might be allowed but won't be of much use.

Account Settings

What can I change on my profile

As a user, you can change most details except the email attached to the account. There is a fixed user ID that serves as an identifier for your account. This user ID cannot be changed.

Do vendors see all my details?

Vendors are restricted from seeing sensitive information about you.

How do I subscribe to a product/service?
  • After you sign in, click on “get a health subscription”
  • You will see a list of subscriptions to choose from
  • Click on your product/service of choice
  • Read the details of the subscription including terms and conditions.
  • Pay for the subscription
  • Enjoy!

How do I cancel my subscription?

 Click the “-” icon at the top right corner of the selected subscription tray. You will be asked if you are sure of your action. Click yes and the subscription will be successfully canceled.

Discover

Can I learn about productivity and health-related topics?

 With the discover button, you can access articles on productivity tips and health-related topics. It is all about helping you get the right fit for your health goals.

Vendor

How do I sign up to be a vendor?

 Visit www.heltapp.io then click on the Signup button. Fill in the details and click Submit Our support team will follow up on your request and after that, you can officially become a vendor

How do vendors get paid?

 When the vendors have been verified, set up, and earning, a vendor can withdraw money once a week direct to their bank accounts by clicking the “withdraw button”.

What is the verification phase like?

 After receiving a vendor request, we contact the vendors, fix a meeting, and plan for a verification exercise. Based on our criteria, Helt accepts or declines a vendor’s request. The criteria differ depending on the business of the vendor.
